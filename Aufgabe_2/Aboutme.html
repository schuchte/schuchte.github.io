About me

Hi Guys.

My name is Tanja Schuchter and I am 19 years old.
I live in Germany, more precisely in a small village near Waldshut- Tiengen.
It's pretty close to the border of Swizerland.

After my graduation in 2018, I decided to make something meaningful.
So I started working in an aftercare clinic for children suffering from cancer.
And I realized soon that this decision was a life- enhancing one.
My life has been kind of enlightened.
To see the smiles of these kids was suddenly the most beautiful thing in the world.
It wasn't just a smile, it was a present.
A present which meant a lot to me.
And even though I firstly questioned that choice due to some self- doubt and mental pressure, I would never reverse this detemination.

But coming back to the main issue, I have to mention further information about my person.

I like to spend time with friends or family.
I prefer both, doing adventures togehter such as diving, or spending time on banalities like having dinner or watching a movie.
I am thankful for every moment we can share.

Besides I enjoy to paint, draw, play guitar, listen to music, dance, play football or doing winter sports such as skiing and snowboarding.
And there is one hobby which turned into something very important to me:

POETRIE.

Composing Poems means to reflect yourself in a creative way, to express your thoughts, your feelings, your whole view of life.

It's kind of an individual fulfilment.
It's like Art consisting of words...

Currently you saw my first Web- Portfolio, which is exactly about that kind of art.
I hope you enjoyed.

best regards, Tanja.
